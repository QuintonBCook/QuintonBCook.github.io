Germany

Rotary International has a program for highschoolers called Rotary Youth Exchange(RYE). Through this program Quinton went to Berlin Germany, rotarty district 1940. He lived there for eight months before being sent home due to the COVID-19 pandemic. He had three host families who lived in very different places even though they were in the same city. One lived in the middle, near Alexanderplatz, and the other two lived farther out in the suburbs of Berlin. He went to the Georg-Herwegh-Gymnasium, a highschool in the Hermsdorf region of Berlin.

The Journey

Quinton was first introduced to the idea of foreign exchange in the fifth grade, when his family hosted a girl from Germany. She stayed with them for a whole year and has they have visited eachother periodically.

He left on the 27th of July in 2019 and arrived with his first host-family, the Kleileins. Their son also studied abroad in Peru.
