Service Learning Project

The Canadian Independ School District recently hired a new technology teacher at their middle school. In prior the curriculum for this class included a 3D printing section. However, this new teacher had not ever word with CAD or 3D printers.
Quinton offered his services to teach the teacher the basics of both CAD and 3D printing. He did this by showing the teacher how to model a maker coin in Fusion 360. They then printed it out of PLA using a QiDi-XPlus 3D Printer.
