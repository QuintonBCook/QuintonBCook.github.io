IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, or IEEE, is an organization specifically for electrical engineers. There is a fee to join IEEE unless you are a attending a college level class that will wave that fee. Quinton Cook plans on joining IEEE in the Spring of 2022 as he will begin taking college level engineering classes at Texas Tech University

ROTEX INTERNATIONAL

Rotex International, or simply Rotex, is a group of rotary exchange student alumn. Rotex are often mentors to incoming and outgoing exchange students and use their own knowledge and experiences to guide new exchange students. Quinton joined this organization a few months after his exchange, which ended in March of 2020. Because of the pandemic the Rotary exchange program has been halted, but plans on returning in 2022.
